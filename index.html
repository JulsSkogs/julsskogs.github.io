My Interests

Cooking

I have been off-and-on with meal prepping for a few years, but lately I have developed a real taste for home cooking! I've made my own kombucha and vinegars, and would like to start trying to make beer. I've also gotten much better at cooking in general, which is always nice! It seems like just about every day at this point I run across a new recipe or technique video that I want to try out, and it's tons of fun.

Gardening

In 2022, my fiancee and I decided to plant an herb and fruit/vegetable garden in our back yard! We planted brussel sprouts, broccoli (which unfortunately didn't make it), bell peppers, lettuce, three types of tomato, onions, artichoke and banana peppers. Our fruits are strawberries, raspberries, blue berries and rhubarb (though I suppose that's a veggie) and for herbs we went with mint, lavendar, rosemary, cilantro and dill.

Coding

I am currently engaging in self-paced online courses to help me learn to code! I am hoping to, in the future, move into web development as a career in the not too distant future. The learning journey has been super fun, sort of like adult LEGO, and the challenges have been surprisingly enjoyable to overcome. I have also set up my own media PC/Plex server that lives on a Synology DS420+, having setup most of the programs I use through Docker containers. While frustrating at times, that was another very fun learning experience that was well worth it in the end.

Gaming
